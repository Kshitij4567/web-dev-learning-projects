Paneer Butter Masala

About Recipe | Channel | More Recipies

A rich creamy North indian dish made with paneer,tomato, aromatic species

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • spices: garam masala, Turmeric, chilli Powder

Instructions

  • Heat butter in pan and saute onions
  • Add tomatoes and cook until soft
  • Blend the mixture into smooth gravy
  • Add spices and paneer cubs
