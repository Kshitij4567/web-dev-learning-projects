CSS Position Practice

Static

Static: Default position, apni normal jagah par rehta hai.

Relative

Relative: Apni jagah se shift hota hai, lekin space occupy karta hai.

Absolute

Absolute: Parent (relative) ke hisaab se place hota hai.

Sticky

Sticky: Scroll karte waqt top par chipak jaata hai.

Fixed

Fixed: Scroll karne par bhi screen ke corner me chipka rehta hai.
